Honey, I Granulated the Kids

This piece is inspired by aspects of acoustic ecology; including our relationship to environmental sound, the nature of soundscapes and and ecological approaches to composition and material - as described by Keller (2000).

Granular synthesis techniques are used to stretch a 40-second field recording of children playing over 12 minutes in various ways. The layered results are further prodded and pruned to accentuate inherent musicalities and inspire new directions.

A more detailed description of my compositional approach can be found here.
